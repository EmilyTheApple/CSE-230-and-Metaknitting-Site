Patterns

Fish Hat Pattern

This pattern was originally created by Thelma Egberts and posted to the Knitty website. The pattern can be adapted for straight needles or knitting in the round with some optional shaping for the mouth. Check it out and create your own color-customized head accessory. Variations can also be made for the eyes, fins, and tails. This pattern is super fun for someone not afraid of unique, alternate head covers.

Cat Ear Hat Pattern

There are many variations of patterns for this hat online, but this pattern is the closest to my own pattern. The cat ear hat can either be worked in the round with no decreasing or shaping and then seamed at the top in one line or it can be knitted like a small scarf with two straight seams on the side. Additional stitches can be placed to shape the ears.

Bearded Hat Pattern

The bearded hat is a little more intensive to design and create. Any hat pattern can be used, but this pattern is the best for showing how to shape your beard. Once you've got your base, you can leave it short, crochet on some more texture, or weave long pieces of yarn in to create a long beard. Different types of mustaches can also be used.
